Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. Still curious? WhatsApp us anytime.

What are the requirements to rent a car?
Driver must be 25 or older with a valid driver's license (any country, Latin alphabet) and a major credit card in their own name. We may request a refundable security deposit at pickup.
Is insurance included?
Yes — basic collision and third-party coverage are included on every rental. Additional protection options can be added on request.
Where can I pick up the car?
Miami International Airport (MIA), Fort Lauderdale Airport (FLL), Port of Miami, Port of Fort Lauderdale, or your hotel/residence. We meet you at arrivals — no shuttle, no walk, no wait.
How do I pay?
We accept all major credit cards. The card must be in the primary driver's name. A refundable security deposit is held at pickup and released after the car is returned.
Can I cancel or change my reservation?
Yes. Free changes/cancellations up to 24 hours before pickup. Within 24 hours, a small fee may apply. Just message us on WhatsApp — fastest way to update your booking.
Are there any hidden fees?
No. The quote we send you on WhatsApp includes all taxes, insurance, and airport fees. The price you see is the price you pay.
What if my flight is delayed?
We track your flight. Whether you land early or late, we'll be there. No extra charge for flight delays.
Do you offer long-term rentals?
Yes — weekly and monthly rates are available with a discount. Mention your dates in the inquiry form and we'll send a custom quote.
